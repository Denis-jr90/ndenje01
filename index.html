MESSAGE FROM THE PRESIDENT OF CONVOCATION

"Ladies and gentlemen, esteemed guests, and graduates of the Eastern Africa Statistical Training Center (EASTC), it is my honor to address you on this momentous occasion. Today, we gather to celebrate your hard work, dedication, and achievements. As statisticians, you play a crucial role in shaping data-driven decisions across our region. The EASTC has been a beacon of excellence, fostering knowledge, research, and collaboration. Our graduates go seventh equipped with the skills to transform data into meaningful insights. Let this convocation be a reminder that your journey doesn't end here; it's a stepping stone toward a future where data empowers progress. Congratulations, graduates! May your statistical endeavors continue to illuminate the path ahead." " by the power vested in me,as the EASTC Rector, I now constitute and call upon this gathering to be an official academic forum and therefore declare this is the 7th Convocation of the Eastern Africa Statistical Training Center on this 13th day of December 2024" I also declare that this day marking the sixth Convocation is the day to award prizes to all deserving parties that have been identified.

About EASTC

The Eastern Africa Statistical Training Center (EASTC) is a leading institution dedicated to advancing statistical knowledge and expertise across the region. Established with a vision to empower statisticians, researchers, and policymakers, EASTC plays a pivotal role in shaping data-driven decision-making.

At EASTC, our mission is to provide high-quality statistical education and training, foster research and innovation in statistical methodologies, and collaborate with national and international partners to enhance statistical capacity.
